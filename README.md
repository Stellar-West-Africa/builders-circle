# Stellar WA Builders Circle

Community of developers in West Africa building projects on Stellar.

## About the Program

The Builders Circle is a vetted community of developers working on independent projects within the Stellar ecosystem. This program provides:

- **Technical Support**: Weekly syncs, monthly 1-on-1 reviews, and 24/7 assistance
- **Visibility**: Project showcases and contributor spotlights
- **Incentives**: Monthly prizes, quarterly raffles, and merch

## Contribution Tracking

Contributions to Stellar WA repositories earn points toward the leaderboard:

- **Easy**: 1 point (`good-first-issue`, `difficulty:easy`)
- **Medium**: 3 points (`difficulty:medium`)
- **Hard**: 5 points (`difficulty:hard`)
- **Expert**: 8 points (`difficulty:expert`)
- **No label**: Subject to review by the team

Points contribute to monthly prizes and quarterly raffles. [Learn more â†’](./CONTRIBUTING.md)
